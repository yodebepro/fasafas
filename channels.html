Message Scheduling

Write a message now and schedule it to send at the perfect time — across timezones, outside business hours, or as a recurring weekly announcement.

How to Schedule a Message
  1. Write your message in the composer
    Full markdown, file attachments, and emoji supported
  2. Click the caret (∨) next to the Send button
    Reveals 'Schedule message' option
  3. Choose a date and time
    Timezone shown and adjustable. Quick options: Tomorrow 9 AM, Next Monday 9 AM, Custom.
  4. Click 'Schedule'
    Message queued. A 'Scheduled' badge appears below the channel name in the sidebar.
Managing Scheduled Messages
  • View all scheduled messages: channel header → ⏰ Scheduled
  • Edit: click the message → Edit → change text or attachments (not the time)
  • Reschedule: click the clock → pick a new time
  • Cancel: delete the scheduled message before it fires
  • Scheduled messages visible only to you — recipients don't see them until delivery
  • Admin can view all scheduled messages account-wide in Admin Panel → Messaging
Common Use Cases
Async & Timezone Work
  • Write a message at 11 PM, schedule delivery at 9 AM recipient's timezone
  • Schedule Friday updates to land on Monday morning
  • Avoid notifications during team members' off-hours
Recurring Announcements
  • Weekly team standup reminder every Monday 8:55 AM
  • Monthly company update on the first weekday of each month
  • Daily digest posting from an AI agent
