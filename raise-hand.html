Assign Participants

Three flexible assignment modes let you control exactly how participants are distributed across rooms — randomly, with full control, or by letting participants choose for themselves.

Automatic Assignment
FasaFas divides participants evenly and randomly across all rooms. Fastest option for large groups.
How It Works
  • FasaFas calculates: total participants ÷ number of rooms = participants per room
  • Shuffles participants randomly for fair mixing
  • Excludes host and co-hosts (they stay in main session)
  • Excludes panelists (webinar mode)
  • Click Shuffle to re-randomize before opening rooms
  • Preview each room's occupants before opening
  • Works even if some participants haven't joined yet — they'll be assigned when they join
Best For
  • Icebreakers and team mixing activities
  • Random group formation for fairness
  • Large sessions where manual sorting isn't practical
  • Speed networking rounds
  • Any scenario where you want unbiased grouping
Manual Assignment
Drag participants into specific rooms, or use the dropdown to assign each one individually. Full control over every placement.
How to Manually Assign
  1. Open Breakout Rooms panel → choose Manually
    All participants appear in an unassigned list on the right. Each room is shown as a column on the left.
  2. Drag a participant into a room
    Click and drag any participant name into the target room column. Or click the participant's name → Assign to → pick a room from the dropdown.
  3. Move between rooms
    Drag a participant from one room to another at any time — before or after rooms are open. The move is instant.
  4. Check the unassigned list
    Any participant not yet placed appears in the Unassigned section at the top. They stay in the main session until assigned.
Moving Participants After Rooms Are Open
You can reassign participants while rooms are actively running. Click their name in the Breakout Rooms panel, click the current room name, and select a different one. They're moved immediately — their video and audio reconnect in the new room within seconds.
Self-Select Mode
Participants see a list of all rooms and their current occupants. They click Join next to the room they want to enter. Perfect for sessions with topic tracks.
Participant View
  • A dialog appears when rooms open: "Choose a Breakout Room to Join"
  • Each room listed with current number of participants
  • Optional: participant names visible (host configures)
  • Click Join next to preferred room
  • Participants can switch rooms freely (if host allows)
  • Participants can return to main session and rejoin same or different room
  • If a room is full (if cap set), "Room Full" shown — must choose another
Host Controls in Self-Select
  • Host can still manually move any participant
  • Host can lock a participant into a room (prevents switching)
  • Set a capacity limit per room (optional)
  • Enable/disable "show participant names" in room list
  • Allow or restrict room switching after initial choice
  • Close rooms works the same — everyone returned to main
CSV Pre-Assignment Import
Upload a spreadsheet before the meeting to pre-populate all room assignments. Great for classes, workshops, and events where groups are planned in advance.
CSV File Format
Pre-assign Room Name,Email Address
Group A,alice@company.com
Group A,bob@company.com
Group B,carol@company.com
Group B,david@company.com
Group C,eve@company.com
  • Column 1: Room name (will auto-create rooms with these names)
  • Column 2: Email address matching the participant's FasaFas account
  • Participants without a match go to unassigned list
  • Download template CSV button in the Breakout Rooms panel
  • Upload via: Breakout Rooms → Import from CSV
  • Works for pre-meeting setup and during live sessions
