Studio Effects & Lighting

Studio Effects enhance your on-camera appearance automatically — fixing poor lighting, smoothing appearance, and adding subtle cosmetic adjustments without heavy editing.

Appearance Effects
  • Touch up my appearance — skin smoothing slider (0–100% intensity)
  • Lip color — subtle lip tint (natural shades only)
  • Eyebrow definition — enhances brow visibility
  • Facial hair styling — subtle beard shaping
  • All effects are processed on-device via WebGL — no video is uploaded for effect processing
  • Preview in real time before joining (Camera Settings panel)
Lighting Effects
  • Adjust for low light (Auto) — AI brightens your face automatically when ambient light is poor
  • Adjust for low light (Manual) — brightness/contrast slider for fine control
  • Works by boosting brightness and reducing noise in the video frame
  • Particularly useful for evening calls, dark home offices, or backlit settings
  • Does not affect other participants' video — only your outgoing feed
AI Low-Light Enhancement
  1. Light level detection
    The model analyzes average luminance across face pixels in each frame. If below threshold (~80 nits), enhancement activates.
  2. Gamma correction applied
    Gamma curve adjusted to boost mid-tones where most facial detail lives — avoids blowing out highlights or crushing shadows.
  3. Noise reduction
    Low-light cameras produce grain. A lightweight temporal denoiser removes grain without smearing facial details.
  4. Real-time adaptation
    Enhancement adjusts dynamically as room lighting changes — if you switch on a lamp mid-meeting, the enhancement reduces automatically.
How to Access Studio Effects
  1. Before joining
    Click the gear icon in the meeting join preview screen → Video → Studio Effects. Preview all effects before the meeting starts.
  2. During a meeting
    Click the caret (∨) next to the Stop Video button → Video Settings → Studio Effects tab.
  3. Adjust intensity
    Each effect has a slider. Drag to set the level. The live preview updates immediately so you can fine-tune without leaving settings.
