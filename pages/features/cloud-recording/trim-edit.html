Trim & Edit Tools

The FasaFas recording editor lets you trim, cut, and clip your recordings non-destructively — the original is always preserved, and edited versions get their own shareable link.

Recording Editing Tools
Trim (Start & End)
  • Open recording in player → click Edit → Trim Mode
  • A timeline with drag handles appears at the start and end
  • Drag the left handle to trim the beginning (e.g., remove 5 min of small talk)
  • Drag the right handle to trim the end (e.g., remove 10 min of after-meeting chat)
  • Preview the trimmed version before saving
  • Click "Save Trimmed Version" — creates a new recording entry; original unchanged
Cut Sections
  • Mark a section to remove: click the scissors icon and drag over the section to cut
  • Preview with the section removed before committing
  • Multiple cuts allowed — remove up to 10 sections per recording
  • Cuts cannot overlap or be smaller than 5 seconds
  • Smart chapter timestamps automatically adjusted after cuts
  • Transcript times adjusted to match the edited video
Non-Destructive Editing
Your original recording is always preserved
Every edit in FasaFas creates a new "edited version" of the recording. The original raw recording is stored separately and never modified. You can create multiple edited versions from the same original — for example, a full version and a highlights clip — and share each independently.
📁 Edit Version Storage
OriginalStored permanently — counts against your storage quota
Edited versionsStored as cut-point metadata — NOT re-encoded, uses minimal storage
Playback of editsSegments assembled on-the-fly during HLS stream — cut sections skipped
Downloaded MP4Re-encoded with cuts applied — full new MP4 file generated on download
Creating Highlight Clips
  • Highlight Clips: create a short excerpt from any section of the recording
  • Select start and end point on the timeline → click "Create Clip"
  • Clip gets its own title, thumbnail, and shareable link
  • Clip is independent from the parent recording — can be shared without sharing the full meeting
  • Add CTA (Call to Action) button to the clip: "Schedule a meeting", "Visit our site", etc.
  • Clips can also be exported to the Clips module as async video messages
  • Analytics: view count, completion rate per clip tracked separately from the full recording
