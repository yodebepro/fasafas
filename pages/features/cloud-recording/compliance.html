Compliance & Legal Hold

FasaFas recordings meet the compliance requirements of regulated industries — with eDiscovery export, legal hold, full audit trails, HIPAA BAA support, and GDPR data subject controls.

eDiscovery and Legal Hold
eDiscovery API
  • Search recordings by date range, participant, keyword in transcript
  • Bulk export matching recordings in a single authenticated API call
  • Export includes: MP4, transcript, summary, participant list, and metadata
  • Available on Business+ with eDiscovery add-on, and Enterprise
  • Used for: litigation holds, regulatory audits, HR investigations
Legal Hold
  • Mark specific recordings as "Legal Hold" — they are exempt from all auto-delete policies
  • Legal hold recordings cannot be deleted by any user (including admins) until hold is lifted
  • Hold status logged in the audit trail with timestamp and admin who applied it
  • Multiple holds can be applied to one recording (each hold is tracked separately)
  • Notify legal team: when a hold is applied, auto-email sent to a configurable legal contact address
Recording Audit Trail
  • Every recording event logged with: timestamp, user ID, IP address, action
  • Logged events include: recording started, stopped, downloaded, shared, deleted, hold applied, hold lifted
  • Viewer access logs: every time someone opens a shared recording link
  • Chain of custody: full provenance log proving the recording has not been tampered with (SHA-256 hash stored at creation)
  • Export audit log: Admin Panel → Audit Log → filter by recording ID → export CSV
  • Retention: audit logs retained for minimum 7 years (configurable up to indefinitely on Enterprise)
  • SIEM integration: Splunk / Datadog connector for real-time audit log streaming
