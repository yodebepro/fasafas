AI Meeting Summary

After every meeting with AI enabled, FasaFas generates a structured summary — covering the meeting's purpose, key decisions, outcomes, and next steps. Ready in minutes, editable before sharing.

How the Summary is Generated
  1. Meeting ends — transcript finalized
    Whisper transcription of the full meeting is assembled with speaker labels and timestamps. This becomes the input to the summary generation.
  2. Claude Sonnet processes the transcript
    Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet receives the full transcript (up to 200,000 tokens — handling meetings up to ~8 hours). A structured prompt instructs it to produce the summary in sections.
  3. Summary generated in 30–90 seconds
    Total pipeline time: transcription + diarization + summary = typically 3–8 minutes after meeting end. Host receives a notification when ready.
  4. Host reviews and edits before sharing
    The summary is shown to the host with an "Edit" mode. Names, decisions, and figures can be corrected before the summary is published or shared.
What the Summary Contains
📋 Standard Summary Sections
Meeting OverviewTopic, date, attendees, duration, purpose of the meeting
Key Discussion Points3–5 bullet summary of major topics covered
Decisions MadeNumbered list of confirmed decisions extracted from transcript
Action ItemsTask, assignee (if mentioned), due date (if mentioned)
Next StepsForward-looking items and upcoming milestones discussed
Open QuestionsQuestions raised that were not conclusively answered
Sharing and Templates
Sharing Options
  • Share via link (view-only, no account needed)
  • Push to Slack channel: one-click, includes summary text + recording link
  • Email draft: pre-composed email with summary text — send directly from FasaFas
  • Export to Doc: creates a Docs page with the full summary and action items
  • Push action items to Tasks (one-click for all items at once)
  • Webhook: summary pushed to your endpoint on meeting.summary.completed event
Custom Templates
  • AI Companion add-on: 8 custom summary templates (e.g., Sales Call, Sprint Review, 1:1, Board Meeting)
  • Custom AI add-on: unlimited custom templates with your own section structure
  • Template example — Sales Call: includes Prospect Name, Pain Points, Next Steps, Deal Stage
  • Admins can set organization-wide default summary template
  • Templates applied at meeting creation time or retroactively from recordings
