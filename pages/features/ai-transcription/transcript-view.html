Transcript Viewer Features
During the Meeting
  • Opens as a scrollable panel alongside the video
  • Auto-scrolls to follow the live transcription as it appears
  • Click any line → audio jumps to that moment (if host enabled it)
  • Your own speech highlighted in a distinct color
  • Search bar at top — results highlighted inline
  • Time-lock toggle: pause auto-scroll to read a specific section
Post-Meeting Viewer
  • Full transcript shown alongside the recording player
  • Transcript line highlighted in real time as recording plays
  • Click any transcript line → player jumps to that timestamp
  • Speaker filter: show only one speaker's lines
  • Chapter filter: show only lines from a specific smart chapter
  • Add comments to any transcript line (timestamped)
  • Flag lines for review ("this seems incorrect — edit")
Search Capabilities
  • Keyword search: highlights all occurrences across the full transcript
  • Jump between results: ← → arrows navigate through matches
  • Phrase search: wrap in quotes for exact phrase — "budget approved"
  • Speaker filter: search within one speaker's lines only
  • Time range filter: only show lines within a specific time window
  • Search from Hub: search across ALL your past meeting transcripts at once
  • AI semantic search: "find where we discussed the Q3 forecast" — finds relevant sections even without keyword match
  • Results ranked by relevance (semantic search) or chronological (keyword)
