Privacy & Consent

FasaFas gives participants, hosts, and administrators full control over AI transcription data — including consent flows, individual opt-out, data deletion, and regional storage.

User Controls
Admin Controls
Consent and Notification
  • When AI transcription starts, ALL participants see a banner: "This meeting is being transcribed by AI"
  • The banner is mandatory — admins cannot suppress it (regulatory requirement)
  • Participants who join after transcription starts receive the notice immediately on join
  • Audio announcement (configurable): "This meeting is being recorded and transcribed" plays when transcription begins
  • Consent prompt (optional, admin setting): participants must click "I Agree to AI Transcription" before audio is processed
  • If a participant doesn't consent (when prompt is enabled): their audio is excluded from the transcript
  • Transcription indicator always visible in the meeting UI while active
Individual User Controls
  • Request exclusion: a participant can click "Opt out of transcription" — their audio is no longer processed by AI for the remainder of the meeting
  • Delete voice registration: Profile → Smart Recognition → Delete Voice Data (clears your enrolled voice embedding)
  • Request transcript deletion: contact account admin or submit via GDPR portal → specific meeting transcript deleted within 30 days
  • Download your data: Profile → Privacy → Download My Data includes all transcripts where you were a participant
  • View where your voice data is stored: Profile → Smart Recognition → Storage Region
Admin Privacy Controls
Account-Wide Settings (Admin Panel → AI Settings)
  • Enable / disable AI transcription account-wide (master toggle)
  • Require participant consent prompt before transcription starts
  • Set transcript retention period: 30 days / 90 days / 1 year / 7 years / custom
  • Opt account out of AI model training — data never used to improve any AI model
  • Data residency: select which AWS region stores transcripts (US / EU / APAC)
  • Disable Smart Recognition voice storage for all users
  • Restrict transcript download: host-only download toggle
  • Audit log: every transcript access, download, share, and deletion logged with timestamp and IP
  • GDPR: "Purge All Transcripts for User" tool — removes all transcript data attributed to a specific user
