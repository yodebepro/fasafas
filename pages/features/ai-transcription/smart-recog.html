Smart Recognition

Smart Recognition maps the automatic 'Speaker 1, Speaker 2' labels to real participant names by matching live speech against enrolled voice embeddings — fully opt-in and stored encrypted.

Voice Enrollment Process
  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Smart Recognition
    Toggle "Enroll Voice for Smart Recognition". This is an opt-in feature — off by default.
  2. Read the enrollment script aloud (30 seconds)
    A short passage is displayed. Read it clearly in your natural speaking voice. The system needs enough audio to build an accurate voice embedding.
  3. Voice embedding generated on-device
    A 192-dimensional vector representation of your voice characteristics is computed locally. This is a mathematical fingerprint — NOT a recording of you speaking.
  4. Embedding encrypted and stored in your account
    The embedding is encrypted with your account key before being uploaded. FasaFas staff cannot listen to or reconstruct any speech from it.
Live Name Matching During Meetings
  • As audio segments arrive, the diarization engine extracts a voice embedding per speaker segment
  • Each segment embedding is compared against all enrolled embeddings in the account using cosine similarity
  • Match threshold: 0.85 cosine similarity (tunable by admin — lower = more matches, higher = stricter)
  • If a match is found, the speaker label is updated from "Speaker 2" to "Marcus Smith" retroactively
  • Matching improves as more of the meeting passes — a 30-second segment gives better accuracy than a 3-second one
  • Unmatched speakers retain the "Speaker X" label in the final transcript
Privacy and Data Controls
  • Opt-in only — never automatic enrollment from meeting audio
  • Voice embeddings are mathematical vectors, not audio recordings — cannot be reversed to speech
  • Stored encrypted at rest (AES-256) with account-specific key
  • Never shared with other accounts or used to train AI models
  • Delete your enrollment: Profile → Smart Recognition → Delete Voice Data
  • Admin can disable Smart Recognition feature account-wide
  • GDPR: voice data included in "download my data" and "delete my data" requests
  • Matching only runs against enrolled users in the same account — no cross-account matching
