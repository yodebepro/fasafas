FedRAMP Moderate Authorization
  • FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program): the US government's standardized cloud security authorization framework
  • Authorization level: Moderate — appropriate for systems handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)
  • Authorization path: 3PAO (Third-Party Assessment Organization) assessment + ATO (Authority to Operate) from a sponsoring agency
  • Annual assessment: continuous monitoring with annual full re-assessment
  • Accessible in the FedRAMP marketplace: search 'FasaFas' at marketplace.fedramp.gov
  • Available only on FedRAMP GovCloud instance — separate from commercial FasaFas

Federal Procurement
  • Available via GSA Schedule 70 (IT Schedule 70) contract vehicle
  • DUNS/CAGE code: available from your FasaFas federal account rep
  • FedRAMP package: security package available to agencies through the FedRAMP secure repository
  • Agency ATO reuse: agencies can issue their own ATO leveraging FasaFas's FedRAMP authorization (inherit controls)
  • Custom terms: agencies may negotiate custom DPA and data handling terms
  • Contact: federal@fasafas.app for GovCloud pricing and procurement support
