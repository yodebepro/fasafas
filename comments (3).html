Embed Recordings & Whiteboards

Embed FasaFas meeting recordings, whiteboards, Figma frames, YouTube videos, and more directly into any doc — content plays or renders inline without leaving the document.

Meeting Recordings
How to Embed
  1. Type /embed in the doc or paste a URL on a blank line
  2. FasaFas detects the URL type and offers a rich embed option
  3. Click 'Embed' → content renders inline as an interactive card
  4. Resize: drag the bottom edge of the embed to adjust height
  5. Caption: click below the embed → add a description or attribution
  6. Click embed to open full-screen: viewer can expand to full-screen within the doc

Embedding Meeting Recordings
  • After a meeting, the recording is available in Hub → Recordings within 5 minutes
  • In any Doc: /embed → paste the recording URL → inline player appears
  • Player shows: video on left, AI transcript on right (synchronized)
  • Click any transcript line → video jumps to that timestamp
  • Comments on the recording embed: click a timestamp → leave a timestamped comment
  • Share: anyone with Doc access can view the embedded recording if they also have recording access
