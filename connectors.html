Pen, Highlighter & Eraser

Freehand drawing tools for annotation, emphasis, and real-time explanations — with a pen for precise drawing, highlighter for emphasis, and a laser pointer that fades automatically.

Pen, Highlighter & Eraser
✏️ Drawing Tools
PenFreehand vector path — stays on canvas after drawing. Width 1–20px, any color.
HighlighterSemi-transparent strokes for marking up text or shapes. Width 10–40px.
EraserErase pen/highlighter strokes. Pixel eraser or object eraser (deletes entire stroke).
Stroke smoothingEnabled by default — smooths wobbly freehand strokes into clean curves.
Color pickerFull RGB picker + 20 preset colors + 'pick from canvas' eyedropper.
Pen historyAll pen strokes are vector objects — undo/redo like any other object.
Laser Pointer
  • Activate: press L or click the laser pointer tool in the toolbar
  • Click and drag on canvas — a red dot with a fading trail follows your cursor
  • Trail fades after 2 seconds — nothing is left on the canvas
  • All participants see the laser pointer in real time — great for presentations
  • The laser dot shows your name label (so attendees know who's pointing)
  • Use in Follow Mode: presenter's laser is visible to all followers simultaneously
Tablet & Stylus Support
  • FasaFas Whiteboard supports pressure-sensitive stylus input on touch devices
  • iPad with Apple Pencil: stroke width varies with pen pressure — natural drawing experience
  • Android tablets with active stylus: pressure sensitivity supported on Chrome/Edge
  • Palm rejection: enabled automatically — resting your palm on the screen doesn't create strokes
  • Tilt sensitivity: supported on Apple Pencil 2 and Wacom styli — affects stroke angle/width
