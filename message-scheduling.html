Voice Messages

Record and send voice messages instead of typing — with a waveform player, AI-generated transcript, and playback speed control. Great for async communication when text isn't enough.

Recording a Voice Message
  • Click the microphone icon in the message composer (or hold M on keyboard)
  • Recording starts immediately — no countdown
  • Maximum length: 5 minutes per voice message
  • Pause and resume during recording
  • Preview before sending — re-record if needed
  • Cancel: click X to discard without sending
  • Available in channels, DMs, group DMs, and thread replies
Playback Features
  • Waveform visualizer shows amplitude over time
  • Click any point on the waveform to seek to that moment
  • Playback speed: 0.8× / 1× / 1.2× / 1.5× / 2×
  • Background playback: continue listening while scrolling other messages
  • Auto-pause when another voice message or video starts playing
  • Listen counter: shows how many people have listened
AI Transcript
  • AI transcription (Whisper) runs automatically after sending
  • Transcript appears below the waveform player after ~15–30 seconds
  • Click any transcript word to jump to that moment in the audio
  • Search: voice message transcripts are included in channel/DM search
  • Copy transcript to clipboard
  • Transcript also available via API: GET /api/v1/messages/:id/transcript
