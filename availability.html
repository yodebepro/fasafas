Hold, Transfer & Conference

Put calls on hold with custom music, transfer to a colleague with a warm announcement, or add a third party to create a conference call — all from the in-call controls.

Conference Calls
Hold
  • Click the Hold button during an active call
  • Caller hears hold music (default FasaFas music, or custom uploaded in admin)
  • Hold timer shows on your screen — don't leave callers waiting too long
  • Resume: click Resume to reconnect instantly
  • Multiple holds: place the current call on hold and take a second incoming call simultaneously
  • Park: transfer a call to a 'parked' extension — any team member can pick it up by dialing the park slot number
Transfer Types
Warm Transfer
  • Put caller on hold → call the target colleague → announce the caller → confirm transfer → connect
  • Caller never hears you consulting the colleague
  • Target colleague is briefed before the call lands with them
  • Best practice for customer service handoffs
Cold (Blind) Transfer
  • Immediately transfer the call to another number or extension without consulting first
  • The caller is connected to the target without any briefing
  • Faster but less personal — appropriate for internal routing
  • The target's voicemail activates if they don't answer within the configured timeout
Conference Calls
  • During an active call → click 'Add Call' → dial a third number
  • Three-way call: you + caller A + caller B all connected simultaneously
  • Merge: if you have two active calls (one on hold), click Merge → creates conference
  • Maximum 3 parties on a standard voice conference call
  • Escalate to video: click 'Elevate to Meeting' → converts to a full FasaFas video meeting with screen share
  • Conference recording: same consent rules as standard call recording
