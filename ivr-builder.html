AI Virtual Receptionist

An AI-powered virtual receptionist that handles inbound SMS messages automatically — understanding intent, answering common questions, and routing complex requests to the right human.

Auto-Reply SMSNLP IntentBooking LinksOff-HoursNo Human Needed
How It Works
Capabilities
Setup
AI Virtual Receptionist
  1. Inbound SMS received on your business number
    Any time of day — during or outside business hours
  2. AI reads the message and classifies intent
    Intent categories: booking request, question, complaint, info request, sales inquiry, other
  3. AI generates a relevant auto-reply
    Based on intent + your configured business information + FAQ answers
  4. Routes complex requests to a human
    If AI confidence <80% or if the visitor requests to speak to a human: thread routed to the next available agent with AI summary prepended
  5. Full conversation log available
    Human agent sees the full AI conversation + can reply directly from the SMS thread
AI Receptionist Capabilities
What It Handles
  • Answer frequently asked questions (hours, location, pricing, services)
  • Book appointments: share your Scheduler booking link
  • Collect lead information: name, company, email → pushed to CRM
  • Handle Spanish/French/other languages (auto-detected)
  • Take a message and notify the right team member
  • Respond to 'Is someone available?' with real-time agent availability status
Handoff to Human
  • Triggered by: customer says 'speak to a human', complex complaint, low AI confidence
  • Seamless: agent receives thread with AI conversation summary
  • Agent reply appears to come from the same business number — seamless for the customer
  • After-hours: collect message and schedule callback for next business day
Setup
  1. Admin Panel → Phone → AI Receptionist → Enable
  2. Add your business info: hours, location, services, pricing FAQs
    AI uses this as its knowledge base for answering questions
  3. Upload a FAQ document (optional — up to 50 Q&A pairs)
    AI reads these to answer domain-specific questions
  4. Set handoff rules: when to escalate to a human
  5. Test: send a test SMS to your number → review AI responses
  6. Activate: AI handles inbound SMS immediately
