Daily Digest

Every morning FasaFas compiles a personalized digest — yesterday's meeting summaries, open action items, overdue tasks, today's upcoming meetings, and AI-filtered channel highlights — in one brief.

What's in the Daily Digest
📊 Digest Sections
Yesterday's MeetingsBullet summary of each meeting: decisions + key outcomes
Open Action ItemsAll pending tasks from meetings — with due dates and source links
Overdue ItemsHighlighted separately — tasks past their due date
Today's MeetingsUpcoming calendar: topic, time, attendees, pre-read links
Unread Channel HighlightsAI-filtered important messages from your followed channels
AI Suggested FocusOne priority item AI recommends for today — based on calendar + open items
Delivery Options
In-App (Hub)
  • Appears as a card at the top of Hub when you open FasaFas
  • Interactive: click action item → opens task; click meeting → opens summary
  • Dismissable: archive once read
  • Available on web, desktop, and mobile
Email
  • Delivered to your email at a configured time (default: 8:00 AM local)
  • HTML email with clickable links per item
  • Reply to email → creates a note in your Hub
  • One-click unsubscribe from any digest email
Customize Your Digest
  • Enable/disable each section individually: Settings → AI Companion → Daily Digest
  • Delivery time: 6 AM, 7 AM, 8 AM, or custom
  • Timezone: auto-detected from profile, overridable
  • Delivery days: weekdays only (default) or every day
  • Channel sources: choose which channels to include in 'Unread Highlights'
  • Admin can enable/disable digest account-wide or set required delivery time
  • Available on: AI Companion add-on and above
