Dial Pad with Contact Lookup

A full dial pad with instant contact search, recent call history, favorites list, and international dialing — accessible from any FasaFas app in one click.

Dial Pad Features
  • Access: click the phone icon in the left nav or press Alt+P (Windows)
  • 12-key dial pad for manual number entry
  • Contact search: start typing a name → matches from contacts and company directory
  • Recents tab: last 50 calls with name, number, direction (inbound/outbound), and duration
  • Favorites: star any contact → appears in Favorites tab for one-click calling
  • Voicemail tab: visual voicemail list with AI transcript preview
Contact Lookup
  • Company directory: auto-populated from all user accounts in your workspace
  • Personal contacts: synced from Google Contacts or Outlook (optional)
  • CRM contacts: if Salesforce or HubSpot is connected — their records appear in search
  • Search by: name, extension number, job title, department
  • One-click call: find the contact → click the phone icon next to their name
  • Contact card: shows presence status, timezone, and recent meeting history
International Dialing
  • Dial international numbers: include country code (e.g., +44 for UK, +33 for France)
  • Country picker: click the flag icon → select country → auto-fills country code
  • International call rates apply based on your plan's included minutes
  • View per-country rates: Admin Panel → Billing → Call Rates
  • Admin can restrict international dialing by user group (e.g., only executives can call premium-rate countries)
  • Emergency numbers: always routed via local PSTN regardless of account settings
