Elevate Call to Video Meeting

Turn any voice call into a full FasaFas video meeting with one click — camera, screen sharing, recording, and all meeting features — without hanging up or sending a new invitation.

One ClickFull VideoScreen ShareNo New LinkAutomatic Invite
How Elevation Works
What the Caller Gets
Requirements
Elevating a Voice Call to Video
  1. During any active call, click 'Video' in the call controls
    The button is in the top call control bar — works for both inbound and outbound calls
  2. FasaFas creates an instant meeting room
    A unique meeting ID and join link are generated in milliseconds
  3. Caller receives an SMS or in-app notification with the meeting link
    They receive: 'Join the video meeting: [link]'
  4. Both parties join the video meeting
    Voice call automatically ends as you both connect to the video meeting
  5. Full meeting features available
    Camera, screen share, recording, AI transcription, breakout rooms — everything
What the Caller Experiences
  • If calling from a mobile phone: receives an SMS with the join link
  • If calling from a FasaFas app: instant in-app notification with 'Join Video Meeting' button
  • If calling from a desk phone: receives an SMS — they open it on their mobile browser
  • Browser-based meeting room: no app download required for the caller
  • Calendar invite is auto-sent if the caller has an email on file in your CRM (Salesforce integration)
