Confirmation & Reminder Emails

Guests automatically receive a confirmation email when they book, a reminder 24 hours before, and another 1 hour before — fully customizable and including a one-click cancellation link.

📧 Default Email Sequence
Instant confirmationSent immediately when booking is confirmed — includes meeting link, Zoom/FasaFas room, calendar attach (.ics)
24-hour reminderDay-before reminder with event details and join link
1-hour reminderFinal reminder — most impactful for reducing no-shows
Custom remindersAdd reminders at any interval: 1 week, 3 days, 2 hours, 15 minutes — any combination
Follow-up emailOptional: send after the meeting with meeting summary or a 'How was it?' survey
Email Customization
  • Custom email templates per booking page — use your own copy and branding
  • Variables: {guest_name} {meeting_title} {meeting_time} {meeting_link} {host_name} — auto-filled
  • HTML editor: custom styling, logo, signature
  • SMS reminders: enable for guests who provide a phone number — sent alongside email
  • Language: emails sent in guest's browser language (if detectable), or your configured default
  • 'From' name: emails appear to come from you (your name via FasaFas's sending infrastructure)
Cancellation & Rescheduling
  • All confirmation emails include: 'Need to cancel or reschedule? Click here'
  • Cancel: one click → you notified → event removed from both calendars
  • Reschedule: guest sees your current availability → picks a new slot → both calendars updated
  • Host cancels: click 'Cancel' on the event → guest receives a cancellation email with optional message
  • Last-minute cancel protection: admin can set a policy that blocks cancellations within 2 hours of the meeting
  • No-show handling: automatic 'You missed our meeting' follow-up (optional toggle)
