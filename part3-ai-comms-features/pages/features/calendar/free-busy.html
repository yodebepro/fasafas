Free / Busy Overlay for Invitees

See your invitees' availability as colored overlays on your calendar before scheduling — AI suggests the best meeting time based on everyone's free/busy without seeing their private event titles.

Privacy Settings
Free/Busy Overlay for Invitees
  • When creating an event: add invitees → their free/busy status overlaid on the time selector
  • Green/white slots: invitee is free
  • Red/dark slots: invitee is busy (another meeting in their calendar)
  • Overlay mode: 'Find a time' view shows all attendees' schedules stacked horizontally
  • Scroll forward/backward to find a slot where everyone is free
  • Works for: FasaFas users (real-time), Google Calendar users (synced), and Outlook users (synced)
AI Best Time Finder
  • After adding all invitees → click 'Suggest Best Time'
  • AI analyzes everyone's free/busy + your stated meeting duration
  • Considers: not too early/late in the day, preferred meeting hours, avoid lunch blocks
  • Returns 3 suggested times ranked by: number of conflicts, time of day preference
  • Click a suggestion → event time pre-filled, one more click to save
  • AI learns from your scheduling patterns — times you consistently accept or reject
What AI Can't See
AI only accesses free/busy status — not event titles, descriptions, or attendees of existing events. Privacy is maintained throughout.
