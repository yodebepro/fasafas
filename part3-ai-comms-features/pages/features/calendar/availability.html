Custom Availability & Buffer Times

Define exactly when you're available for bookings — working hours per day, buffer time between meetings, minimum advance notice, and specific dates to block out.

Configuring Your Availability
⏰ Availability Settings
Working hoursSet open hours per day of week — e.g., Mon–Fri 9 AM–5 PM, Thu closes at 3 PM
TimezoneDefault timezone for your availability — guest sees slots in their timezone
Minimum noticeHow far in advance a meeting must be booked — e.g., 4 hours or 24 hours minimum
Maximum futureHow far out bookings are allowed — e.g., no bookings more than 60 days in advance
Slot duration15 / 30 / 45 / 60 / 90 / 120 minutes — or offer guest a choice
Slot start incrementsEvery 15 min, 30 min, or hour — controls granularity of available slot times
Buffer Times Between Meetings
  • Before buffer: ensure X minutes free before each booked meeting — for prep time
  • After buffer: ensure X minutes free after each meeting — for wrap-up and context switching
  • Both buffers can be different (e.g., 5 min before, 15 min after)
  • Buffers block out your calendar — other meetings can't be scheduled during buffer slots
  • Buffers don't appear on your booking page — guests only see bookable slots that respect buffers
  • Override: manually schedule a meeting into a buffer slot from your own calendar if needed
Availability Overrides
  • One-off override: block out a specific date or date range — for vacation, travel, conference
  • Or: add extra hours on a specific date — e.g., make Saturday bookable just this week
  • Holiday sync: connect to public holiday calendar for your country → auto-blocked
  • Override doesn't affect your regular availability template — only that specific date
  • Booking page reflects overrides in real time — guests immediately see the blocked/open dates
