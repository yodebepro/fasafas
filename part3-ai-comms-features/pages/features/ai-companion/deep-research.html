Deep Research Pipeline
  1. Query decomposition
    GPT-4o breaks your question into 5–12 sub-questions, identifying different facets to research independently
  2. Parallel web search
    Each sub-question triggers a search. Top 3–5 sources per sub-question read in full. Sources ranked by relevance and credibility.
  3. Source reading and extraction
    Full page content fetched for each source. Key claims, statistics, and quotes extracted. Conflicting information flagged.
  4. Synthesis and cross-reference
    Claude Sonnet synthesizes findings, resolves conflicts between sources, identifies information gaps
  5. Structured report generation
    Full report with executive summary, sections, citations, confidence scores, and follow-up questions. Length: 2,000–10,000 words.
