See all your voicemails as a list — with caller name, time, duration, and AI transcript preview — without calling into a voicemail system or listening to messages in order.

Visual Voicemail Interface
  • Access: FasaFas app → Phone → Voicemail tab
  • Messages listed chronologically with: caller name/number, date/time, duration, and first sentence of AI transcript
  • Unread messages shown in bold with a blue dot indicator
  • Sort: by date (default), duration, or caller name
  • Filter: unread only, or by caller
  • Inbox badge count shown on the Phone icon in the left nav
Playback & Management
  • Click any voicemail → plays inline with waveform player
  • Playback speed: 0.5× 1× 1.5× 2× — listen faster to clear your inbox
  • Pause, seek, replay within the player
  • Reply: call back button directly from the voicemail detail
  • Forward voicemail: share to a colleague's voicemail or as a message in a channel
  • Delete: single message or bulk select → delete
  • Archive: keep message but remove from main inbox
