IVR Visual Builder (No-Code)

Build a professional phone menu system with a visual drag-and-drop editor — no coding required. Create multi-level menus, route to teams, play custom greetings, and handle business hours logic.

IVR Visual Builder
  • Access: Admin Panel → Phone → IVR → Create New Flow
  • Canvas-based drag-and-drop editor — same as the Agent Builder
  • Flow starts with an incoming call trigger → ends at a destination (ring user, play message, hang up)
  • Each node is a step: Play Greeting, Wait for Input, Route by Key Press, Transfer, Voicemail
  • Preview your IVR: 'Test with browser' — calls your browser and walks through the menu
  • Deploy: assign to a phone number → active immediately
  • Version history: revert to any previous IVR version

🌳 Node Types
Play GreetingText-to-speech or upload your own MP3. Multiple voice options + 30+ languages.
Wait for DTMF InputWait up to 10 seconds for caller to press a key. Set timeout action.
Route by Key PressIf caller presses 1 → sales queue · press 2 → support · press 0 → operator
Transfer to UserRoute call to a specific user's extension or mobile number
Transfer to QueueRoute to a call queue (sequential, round-robin, or least-idle routing)
Transfer to VoicemailRoute directly to a user's or shared voicemail box
Business Hours CheckBranch by time of day/day of week → open hours vs. after hours
Play & Hang UpPlay a message and disconnect — for closed/holiday greetings
Business Hours Logic
  • Business Hours node: set your open hours per day of week
  • During hours: route to your normal menu or team
  • Outside hours: play after-hours greeting → route to voicemail or on-call number
  • Holiday schedule: add holiday dates → override normal business hours routing
  • Timezone: set per IVR — useful if your team spans timezones
  • 'Closed today' detection: automatically routes to after-hours when all agents are unavailable (based on presence status)
