AI Suggested Replies

AI analyzes the last few messages in a conversation and suggests 3 relevant replies below the message composer — one-click to use, or ignore and type your own.

AI Suggested Replies
  • Suggestions appear below the message composer in DMs and threads (not in main channel)
  • 3 suggestions generated when: you open a DM or thread with new messages
  • Each suggestion is a short, relevant reply based on the last 5–10 messages
  • Click a suggestion → populates the composer → you can edit before sending or send immediately
  • 'Generate more' → fetches 3 additional suggestions if you don't like the first batch
  • Ignore: simply type your own reply and the suggestions fade away
  • Settings → AI Companion → Suggested Replies → toggle off if you prefer not to see them
What Makes Good Suggestions
  • Suggestions match the tone of the conversation (casual chat → casual reply, formal request → formal response)
  • Context-aware: reads conversation history to understand what's being asked
  • Meeting-aware: if the DM references a meeting, AI can incorporate relevant details from the meeting summary
  • Won't suggest a reply if the last message is a file share, link, or reaction (no text to respond to)
  • Won't suggest replies if the conversation is flagged as sensitive (HR policy, legal topics)
Privacy & Opt-Out
  • Suggestions generated on FasaFas servers — conversation text sent to AI model for analysis
  • Admin can disable Suggested Replies account-wide: Admin Panel → AI Settings → Suggested Replies
  • Individual opt-out: Settings → AI Companion → Suggested Replies → off
  • Not available in channels with E2EE enabled
  • Text used to generate suggestions is not stored after the suggestion is shown
