AI Voicemail Transcription & Actions

Every voicemail is automatically transcribed by Whisper and analyzed by AI — extracting action items, flagging urgency, and suggesting a response — so you never miss a critical message.

AI Voicemail Processing
  1. Voicemail recording complete
    Caller hangs up → recording saved to cloud
  2. Whisper large-v3 transcription (30 seconds)
    Full transcript with punctuation — accurate for phone audio quality
  3. AI analysis by Claude
    Reads transcript → determines: urgency level, topic/intent, action required
  4. Results shown in voicemail list
    Urgency badge (🔴 Urgent / 🟡 Normal / ⚪ FYI), topic tag, action item if present
  5. Notification with AI summary
    Push notification shows: caller, urgency, and what they need
Action Item Extraction
  • AI identifies if the voicemail contains a task, request, or follow-up required
  • Example: 'Hi, I need you to send the contract by Friday' → action item: 'Send contract' · due: Friday
  • Action item shown in the voicemail detail view
  • Push to Tasks: one click → task created with caller name, due date, and voicemail link attached
  • Push to CRM: if Salesforce is connected → activity logged against the contact automatically
  • Admin can set: all action items auto-created as Tasks without manual confirmation
Smart Routing Based on AI
  • Urgent voicemails: AI sends push notification immediately regardless of DND settings (configurable)
  • Topic routing: if AI detects 'billing' → route notification to billing team Slack channel
  • Callback suggestion: for sales inquiries, AI suggests the best time to call back (based on your calendar)
  • Spam detection: AI identifies likely robocalls or spam — sends to a separate Spam folder without notification
  • Language detection: voicemail in Spanish → transcript in Spanish, summary optionally in English
