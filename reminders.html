Personal Booking URL

Share a personal booking URL and let invitees choose their own meeting slot from your live availability — no back-and-forth email chains, just a link they click and a slot they pick.

Embed & Share
Your Personal Booking Page
  • URL format: fasafas.app/book/[yourname] — shareable in email, LinkedIn, email signature
  • Displays your real availability based on your calendar free/busy
  • Available time slots shown in the guest's timezone (auto-detected)
  • Guest picks a slot → event created automatically in both calendars
  • Customizable: page title, meeting description, duration options (15/30/60 min), meeting type (video/phone/in-person)
  • Branding: add your photo and a brief bio to the booking page
Guest Booking Experience
  1. Guest opens your booking link
  2. Selects a meeting type (if you offer multiple) and duration
  3. Picks a date → available slots shown (30-min increments by default)
  4. Selects a time slot → form appears for their name, email, and any custom intake questions
  5. Submits → instant confirmation email with meeting link, calendar invite (.ics), and reminder setup
  6. Day before reminder + 1 hour before reminder sent automatically
Embed & Share Your Booking Page
  • Embed in your website: copy the embed code → paste into any HTML page — shows inline booking widget
  • Email signature: add 'Book a meeting: [your link]' — works in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail
  • LinkedIn: add to Contact Info or a post
  • QR code: download a QR code that links to your booking page — for conferences or printed materials
  • Multiple URLs: create different booking pages for different purposes (demos vs support vs introductions) — Business+ plan
