AI Assistant

A persistent conversational AI throughout FasaFas — in your Hub, inside Docs, and alongside recordings. Ask questions, draft content, search past meetings, and get answers grounded in your actual meeting data.

Claude 3.5 SonnetGPT-4oStreamingMeeting ContextMulti-Turn

AI Assistant Capabilities
General Tasks
  • Draft emails, meeting agendas, reports, proposals
  • Rewrite content in different tones (formal, casual, persuasive)
  • Explain complex topics in plain language
  • Translate text between languages
  • Summarize long documents or web pages (paste URL)
  • Generate code, SQL queries, formulas
Meeting-Aware Tasks
  • 'Summarize what was decided in last Tuesday's all-hands'
  • 'Find all action items assigned to Ana this month'
  • 'What did we discuss about the Q3 budget?'
  • 'Draft a follow-up email based on yesterday's meeting'
  • 'Who mentioned the redesign project in the last 2 weeks?'
  • Searches across all meeting transcripts you have access to
Federated Model Routing
🤖 Which Model Handles What
Meeting summariesClaude 3.5 Sonnet — best at long-context synthesis and structured output
In-session Q&A (streaming)GPT-4o — fastest streaming, supports multimodal input
Speech-to-textWhisper large-v3 — best multilingual STT accuracy
Deep researchGPT-4o + web search — multi-step reasoning
On-device (E2EE)Nemotron-Nano — runs locally, no data leaves device
Semantic searchtext-embedding-3-large + pgvector
Access Points
  • Hub sidebar: Click the sparkle icon (✨) in left nav
  • Inside Docs: Press /ai or highlight text → AI Actions menu
  • In meetings: More → AI → opens AI panel alongside video
  • Mobile: AI button in the bottom nav of the FasaFas mobile app
  • Conversation history persists across sessions
  • Export any conversation: markdown, share link, push to Docs
  • Admin can restrict AI access by user group
