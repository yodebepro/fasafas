Round-Robin Team Scheduling

Let prospects or customers book with any available team member — FasaFas distributes bookings equally across the team using round-robin logic, skipping anyone who's currently unavailable.

Round-Robin Team Scheduling
  • Create a Team Booking Page: Calendar → Booking Pages → New → Round-Robin
  • Add team members (up to 50 members on Business+)
  • Single booking URL shared publicly: guests book with 'your team' — no individual selected
  • FasaFas selects the next available team member automatically
  • Guest sees the selected team member's name only after choosing a time slot
  • Meeting is booked directly on that team member's calendar
No Double-Booking Guarantee
FasaFas cross-checks every team member's live calendar before assigning them. If they have a conflict or are in a meeting, they're skipped — ensuring bookings always land on a genuinely available person.
Distribution Logic Options
⚖️ Assignment Algorithms
Equal distribution (default)Tracks bookings per member — always assigns to the person with the fewest recent bookings
Availability priorityAmong available members, picks the one free longest — least interruption to busy members
Priority orderYou set a ranking — highest priority member gets assigned first if available
Weight-basedAssign weights: senior rep gets 2× bookings vs junior rep — for experienced team members
Team Booking Reporting
  • Dashboard: bookings per team member, this week/month
  • Acceptance rate: how many bookings each member confirmed vs cancelled
  • No-show rate per member
  • Average time to respond to leads
  • Export CSV for sales ops reporting
  • Admin can see all bookings and redistribute if needed
