Google Calendar Bidirectional Sync

Connect your Google Calendar and every event syncs bidirectionally — FasaFas events appear in Google, Google events appear in FasaFas, and changes update in both directions in real time.

Bidirectional Google Calendar Sync
  • FasaFas-to-Google: events you create in FasaFas appear in your Google Calendar immediately
  • Google-to-FasaFas: events in your Google Calendar appear in FasaFas calendar view
  • Updates sync in both directions — change the title in Google → FasaFas updates in <30 seconds
  • RSVPs sync: accept an event in Google → your RSVP status updates in FasaFas
  • Event deletion: delete in either app → removed from both (with confirmation)
  • Free/busy: Google events show as busy in FasaFas scheduling (no title shared unless you choose to share)
Setup Steps
  1. Profile → Integrations → Google Calendar → Connect
  2. Complete Google OAuth in popup → grant FasaFas calendar read/write access
  3. Select which Google calendars to sync (primary calendar always synced)
  4. Choose sync direction: bidirectional (default) or FasaFas → Google only
  5. Initial sync: up to 90 days of history pulled in — completes in under 2 minutes
Meeting Link Behavior
FasaFas can optionally replace Google Meet links in synced events with FasaFas links. Configure: Settings → Calendar → Meeting Link Override.
Conflict & Edge Case Handling
  • Simultaneous edit: if you edit the same event in both apps at the same moment — the most recent save wins
  • Deleted in Google while FasaFas is offline: deleted in FasaFas on next sync
  • All-day events: synced correctly, appear as all-day in both apps
  • Private events: synced but only title shown to other FasaFas users viewing your calendar overlay
  • External invites from non-FasaFas users: appear in FasaFas as Google events — RSVP from FasaFas propagates back to Google
  • Google Calendar API rate limits: sync may be delayed up to 2 minutes during Google outages
