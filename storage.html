Auto-Record Setup

Auto-record starts the cloud recording automatically when the meeting begins — so you never forget to hit record for important calls.

How to Enable Auto-Record
Per-Meeting (Schedule Form)
  1. Open Schedule Meeting → scroll to Advanced Options
  2. Set "Auto-recording" to "Record to Cloud"
  3. Save. Recording starts automatically at meeting begin.
Personal Default (Settings)
  1. Go to Settings → Recording
  2. Enable "Automatic Cloud Recording for all my meetings"
  3. All future scheduled and instant meetings will auto-record.
Auto-record respects the host's join
If "Allow participants to join before host" is disabled, auto-recording starts when the host joins — not when the first participant enters the waiting room. This prevents recording a silent waiting room.
Consent and Participant Notifications
  • When auto-recording starts, all participants see a red banner: "This meeting is being recorded"
  • A red recording dot appears in the meeting toolbar for all participants
  • Audio announcement (configurable): "This meeting is now being recorded" plays when recording begins
  • Late joiners: see the recording indicator and hear the announcement immediately on join
  • Mandatory — cannot be suppressed by host or admin (regulatory requirement in most jurisdictions)
  • Participants can leave the meeting if they do not consent to being recorded
  • Recording consent is logged in the meeting audit trail (time + who was present)
Admin-Level Recording Policy
  • Admin Panel → Meeting Controls → "Auto Cloud Recording" — applies to all meetings in the account
  • Override options: all meetings record / host can choose / recording disabled for all
  • Set which recording layouts are available to hosts (e.g., disable Gallery View recording)
  • Restrict participants from starting or stopping recording (host-only control)
  • Set auto-delete policy: recordings deleted after X days automatically
  • Restrict downloading: participants can watch but not download MP4
  • Require passcode on all shared recording links
