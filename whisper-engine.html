My Notes

My Notes is an AI-assisted note-taking sidebar that runs during any meeting — Zoom or third-party via browser extension. Combine your own notes with AI-captured highlights in one unified view.

My Notes Interface
Layout
  • Opens as a panel alongside the meeting video (resizable)
  • Two sections: YOUR notes (top) + AI Highlights (bottom)
  • Your notes: markdown editor — type freely, supports headers, bold, lists, code
  • AI Highlights: auto-populated as the meeting progresses
  • Click any AI highlight → "Add to My Notes" moves it to your section
  • Dismiss any AI highlight you don't want to keep
  • Timestamps auto-added to every entry (click to jump to that moment in recording later)
Organization
  • Tag any note: #decision #action #question #risk #idea #follow-up
  • Tagged notes appear in dedicated sections for quick scanning
  • Star important notes — they appear at the top of your Hub notes list
  • Assign action items from notes directly to Tasks (person + due date)
  • Notes autosave every 10 seconds — no data loss on browser crash
What AI Automatically Captures
  • Decisions: phrases like "we decided", "let's go with", "agreed to", "confirmed" → added with #decision tag
  • Action items: "will take care of", "can you please", "action for [name]", "by [date]" → added with #action tag
  • Questions: open questions raised but not answered → flagged with #question tag
  • Numbers & dates: budget figures, deadlines, milestones → highlighted for attention
  • Name mentions: "[Name] will lead the project" → captured with context
  • AI confidence score shown per capture: high / medium / review-needed
  • "Review needed" items require your confirmation before being saved permanently
Accessing Notes After the Meeting
  • Notes auto-synced to Hub → Summaries → My Notes within seconds of meeting end
  • Searchable across all past notes from all meetings (full-text search)
  • Filter by: tag, date, meeting title, person mentioned
  • Export options: copy to clipboard, push to Docs, email to self, download as PDF
  • Notes linked to the recording: click any timestamped note → recording jumps to that moment
  • Share individual notes with colleagues (via link, with edit or view access)
  • In-person meetings: My Notes works via FasaFas browser extension — transcribes via your device mic (90 min/mo Basic, unlimited Pro+)
