Drive Retrieval

Connect your cloud storage and ask AI questions about your files — AI fetches, reads, and answers from your documents using retrieval-augmented generation, without you needing to open or search anything manually.

Drive Retrieval (RAG) Pipeline
  1. Connect cloud storage (one-time OAuth)
    Authorize FasaFas to read your Google Drive or OneDrive. Read-only access — cannot modify, delete, or share your files.
  2. FasaFas indexes metadata (not content)
    File names, types, modified dates, and owners indexed. Content is only fetched on-demand when you ask a question.
  3. You ask a question
    'What were the conclusions in the Q3 analyst report?' or 'Find the NDA template for enterprise clients.'
  4. Semantic search identifies relevant files
    text-embedding-3-large embeds your query. pgvector similarity search finds top matching files by semantic meaning, not just keywords.
  5. AI reads files and answers
    Relevant content fetched and passed to Claude with your question. Answer includes quotes and references to specific file and section.

Setup & Privacy
  1. Hub → Integrations → Cloud Storage
  2. Click 'Connect Google Drive' or 'Connect OneDrive'
  3. Complete OAuth in popup — grant read-only access
  4. Choose which folders to index (or all)
  5. Initial index completes in 5–30 minutes depending on file count
Privacy
File contents are only fetched when you ask a question — never stored persistently. Admin can restrict Drive Retrieval per group. FasaFas never accesses files outside the folders you explicitly authorize.
