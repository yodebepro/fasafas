Late Joiner Catch-Up

Join a meeting 20 minutes late and get a private AI brief of everything you missed in under 10 seconds — decisions made, action items assigned, and current topic — shown only to you.

Instant BriefPrivate to YouClaude PoweredOne-ClickNo Interruption

Privacy
Late Joiner Catch-Up Flow
  1. You join the meeting late
    FasaFas detects your join time vs meeting start. If you're 3+ minutes late, the catch-up prompt appears.
  2. Banner appears: 'You joined 18 min late'
    Shows only to you: 'Get AI catch-up of what you missed →' — one button, no one else sees it
  3. Claude generates your private brief in under 10 seconds
    Receives transcript from start to your join time. Produces a concise private summary in a popup panel.
  4. Read and join the conversation
    Brief shows: decisions made, action items, current topic, open questions. Dismiss when done — no trace left.
Brief Contents
📋 Catch-Up Brief Sections
Current TopicWhat the group is discussing right now as you join
Key DecisionsWhat was agreed or confirmed before you arrived
Action ItemsTasks assigned — including any assigned to you
Open QuestionsUnresolved questions still being discussed
Key ParticipantsWho has spoken most and their main points
Suggested ContributionAI suggestion for how to join the conversation naturally
Privacy Guarantee
  • Brief shown only to you — no other participant can see it
  • Client-side popup panel — never appears in shared meeting chat
  • Not stored after you dismiss — ephemeral by design
  • Uses the same transcript shared with all participants — no extra data collected
  • Admin can disable feature: Admin Panel → AI Settings → Late Joiner Catch-Up
  • Available on: AI Companion add-on and above
