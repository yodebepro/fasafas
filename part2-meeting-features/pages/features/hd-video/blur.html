Background Blur

Blur your background with a single click. FasaFas uses AI portrait segmentation to detect and separate you from your background — without a greenscreen or special camera.

AI Background Blur — How It Works
  1. Portrait segmentation model runs on your device
    A TFLite ML model analyzes each video frame (30×/sec) and creates a mask — a pixel-level map of "person" vs "background". Runs entirely on-device for privacy.
  2. WebGL applies Gaussian blur to the background mask
    The GPU uses the person mask to apply a Gaussian blur filter only to the background pixels. Your face and body remain sharp.
  3. Edges are feathered for realism
    The boundary between you and the background is softened (feathered) so the blur doesn't have a harsh cutout look. Glasses, hair, and fine details are handled by the model.
  4. The blurred frame replaces your raw camera output
    The processed frame is passed to WebRTC as your video source. All participants see the blurred version — your camera itself is unchanged.
Blur Intensity Levels
🔵 Blur Settings
No blurRaw camera — background fully visible
Subtle blurLight Gaussian blur — background recognizable but soft (σ=8)
Strong blurHeavy blur — background completely indistinct (σ=24), professional look
Which blur level to use?
Subtle blur is great for home offices where you want to acknowledge your environment while reducing visual clutter. Strong blur is ideal for messy or very private backgrounds where you want full concealment without using an artificial background scene.
Getting the Best Blur Results
  • Good front lighting gives the segmentation model cleaner edges — face a window or desk lamp
  • High-contrast outfits (dark clothes against a light wall) help the model distinguish you from background
  • Avoid wearing clothes that match your background color (camouflage effect confuses the model)
  • Glasses are handled well — if edges look odd, try increasing lighting
  • Hair can sometimes bleed into background at the edges — this is a known limitation of portrait models without greenscreen
  • If your device is slow, switch from Strong to Subtle blur to reduce GPU load
