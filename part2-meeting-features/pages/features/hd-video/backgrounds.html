Virtual Backgrounds

Choose from 50+ built-in background scenes, upload your own image or video, or let the AI generate a background from a text description. No greenscreen required.

Virtual Background Options
Built-In Library (50+ Scenes)
  • Office environments: modern, minimalist, executive
  • Outdoor scenes: city, nature, beach, mountains
  • Abstract and artistic patterns
  • Branded FasaFas scenes for professional meetings
  • Seasonal and holiday backgrounds
  • Dark / subtle options for low-distraction calls
  • Animated loops: fireplace, rain window, bokeh particles
Custom Upload
  • Upload JPG or PNG images (max 15 MB each)
  • Upload MP4 video loops for animated backgrounds
  • Crop/resize preview before applying
  • Store up to 50 personal backgrounds in your library
  • AI-Generated: type a description → AI creates a background image
  • Available via More → Virtual Background → Generate with AI
  • Backgrounds sync across devices (Pro+)
Company Branded Backgrounds (Admin)
  • Admin can upload company-branded backgrounds to the account library
  • Branded backgrounds appear in all users' background selector
  • Can be marked as default for new meetings
  • Admin can restrict users from uploading custom backgrounds
  • Admin can restrict animated backgrounds (bandwidth policy)
  • Recommended size: 1920×1080 PNG, under 5 MB for fast loading
  • Admin backgrounds available on Business and Enterprise plans
