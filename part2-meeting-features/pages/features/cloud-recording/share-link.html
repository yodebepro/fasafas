Share Link & Passcode

Share recordings with a single link — optionally protected by passcode, restricted to specific email domains, set to expire automatically, and with download disabled for viewers.

Generating a Share Link
  1. Open the recording in Hub → Recordings
  2. Click "Share" → "Copy Link"
    A shareable URL is instantly generated. The link includes an embedded access token.
  3. Configure access settings (optional)
    Set passcode, expiry date, domain restriction, or download toggle before copying.
  4. Share the URL
    Paste in email, Slack, Notion, or anywhere. The recipient can watch without a FasaFas account (unless you enable "require sign-in").
Access Control Options
🔒 Share Link Settings
Passcode protectSet a 6-character passcode — viewer must enter it before watching
Link expirySet the link to expire after 7 / 30 / 90 / 180 days or a custom date
Disable downloadViewer can watch via browser but cannot download the MP4 file
Require sign-inViewer must have a FasaFas account and be signed in to watch
Domain restrictionOnly viewers with @yourcompany.com email accounts can access
Revoke linkInstantly disable the link — all previous copies stop working
Recording Share Analytics
  • Total views: how many times the recording has been opened
  • Unique viewers: distinct accounts/browsers that accessed it
  • Completion rate: % of viewers who watched to the end
  • Watch heatmap: which parts of the recording were rewatched most
  • Viewer list: names of signed-in viewers (if "require sign-in" is enabled)
  • Geographic distribution: countries where the recording was viewed
  • Time of view: timestamps of each viewing session
  • Download count: how many times the MP4 was downloaded (if downloads enabled)
