Smart Chapters

Smart Chapters divides the recording into named, timestamped sections based on topic changes in the transcript — so anyone can jump directly to the part they need.

How Smart Chapters Are Created
  1. Topic segmentation on the transcript
    After the meeting ends, Claude processes the full transcript looking for significant topic shifts. A "topic shift" is identified when the semantic content changes substantially from the preceding 2–3 minutes.
  2. Chapter boundaries marked
    Each topic shift becomes a chapter boundary. The AI ensures chapters are at least 3 minutes long to avoid too many micro-chapters. A typical 60-minute meeting has 5–10 chapters.
  3. Chapter titles generated
    Claude creates a 2–6 word descriptive title for each chapter. Examples: "Q3 Budget Review", "New Hire Onboarding Plan", "Action Items & Next Steps".
  4. Chapter summaries written
    A 1–2 sentence summary is written for each chapter — enough context to decide whether to watch that section without watching the whole recording.
Displaying Chapters in the Recording Player
Player Integration
  • Chapter markers shown as vertical lines on the recording timeline
  • Hovering a marker shows the chapter title in a tooltip
  • Chapters listed in a sidebar panel on the right of the player
  • Click any chapter title → player jumps to that timestamp instantly
  • Chapter titles also shown as overlay text when the chapter starts during playback
  • Keyboard shortcut: Next Chapter (N) / Previous Chapter (P)
In the Meeting Summary Card
  • Smart chapters listed under the AI summary text
  • Each chapter shows: timestamp, title, 1-sentence summary
  • Click a chapter in the summary → opens recording at that moment
  • Chapters shareable: copy a deep link to any specific chapter
  • Chapters adjust automatically if the recording is trimmed
API and Export

  • GET /api/v1/meetings/:id/summary → includes smart_chapters array
  • Also delivered via meeting.summary.completed webhook
  • Export as JSON, CSV, or as formatted text in summary email
