Call Recording with Consent

Record any call with automatic consent announcements, cloud storage with AI transcription, and legal hold capability — compliant with two-party consent laws when configured properly.

Consent AnnouncementCloud StorageAI TranscriptPause/ResumeLegal Hold

Call Recording Setup
  • Manual recording: click Record during any active call
  • Auto-record: Admin Panel → Phone → Auto-Recording → enable per user or team
  • Consent announcement: plays automatically before recording starts — 'This call may be recorded...'
  • Consent announcement is mandatory in two-party consent jurisdictions (configurable per state/country)
  • Pause recording: click Pause — useful during sensitive parts of a call (reading card numbers, etc.)
  • Resume: click Resume to continue recording
  • Both parties can hear when recording starts/stops via a brief audio chime
AI Post-Processing
  • Whisper large-v3 transcribes the full call within 2–3 minutes of hang-up
  • Speaker diarization separates your voice from the caller's voice
  • AI summary: 2–3 paragraph summary of the call purpose and outcome
  • Action items: tasks extracted from the conversation (e.g., 'I'll send you the contract')
  • AI action items pushed to Tasks module automatically
  • Recording + transcript stored in Hub → Phone → Recordings
Compliance
  • Two-party consent: auto-announcement required in CA, IL, WA, and other states — configure per area code
  • Legal hold: apply hold to any recording — prevents deletion
  • Audit trail: every recording event logged (start, pause, resume, download, delete)
  • HIPAA: call recordings can be HIPAA-compliant with signed BAA (Enterprise)
  • Retention policy: auto-delete after 30/90/180 days (admin configures)
  • eDiscovery: search and bulk-export recordings via API for legal requests
