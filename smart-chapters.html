Rename Rooms

How to Rename a Breakout Room
  1. Open Breakout Rooms panel
    Click the Breakout Rooms button in the meeting toolbar (host or co-host only).
  2. Click the room name
    The default name ("Breakout Room 1") is clickable. A text field appears in place of the label.
  3. Type the new name
    Type your custom name. Max 64 characters. Emojis supported — click the emoji button at the right of the field.
  4. Press Enter or click ✓
    The name updates instantly. All participants in that room see the new name in their room header and the panel. No disruption to the session.
Rename rooms before or after they open
You can rename rooms at any point — before clicking "Open All Rooms" or while rooms are actively running. Participants already in a room see the name update live without any audio or video interruption.

Saving Room Names for Recurring Meetings
  • When you save the meeting as a recurring event, all room names are preserved in the template
  • Next occurrence loads the same room names and participant assignments automatically
  • Edit names before opening rooms if you need to adjust for that session
  • Save a custom breakout setup as a reusable Meeting Template in your account settings
  • Templates are shareable with co-hosts for consistent naming across your team
