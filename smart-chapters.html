My Notes

A two-column AI note-taker that captures decisions, action items, and key moments automatically while you stay engaged in the conversation — available in FasaFas and any third-party meeting via browser extension.

My Notes Interface
Two-Column Layout
  • Top: your personal typed notes — free-form markdown editor
  • Bottom: AI Highlights — auto-populated as meeting progresses
  • Click any AI highlight → Add to My Notes to pin it
  • Dismiss unwanted AI suggestions
  • Every entry auto-timestamped — click later to jump to recording moment
  • Autosaves every 10 seconds — no data loss on crash
Tag System
  • #decision #action #question #risk #idea #follow-up
  • AI auto-tags based on content type detected
  • Tagged notes organized in collapsible sections
  • Star important notes → surface at top of Hub notes list
  • Push action items to Tasks with one click — assignee + due date pre-filled
  • Sync to Hub → Summaries → My Notes immediately after meeting
What AI Captures Automatically
  • Decisions: 'we decided', 'let's go with', 'agreed to', 'confirmed' → #decision tag
  • Action items: 'I'll take care of', 'can you please', '[name] will', 'by [date]' → #action tag
  • Open questions: raised but not conclusively answered → #question tag
  • Numbers & dates: budget figures, deadlines, milestones → highlighted for review
  • Name mentions: '[Name] will lead the project' → captured with context
  • Confidence score per capture: High / Medium / Review-needed
  • AI learns from your accept/dismiss behavior — personalizes over time
Available in 3rd-Party Meetings
Install the FasaFas browser extension and My Notes runs inside Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, or any in-browser conference. Transcribes via your device mic (90 min/month on Basic, unlimited on Pro+).
Accessing Notes After the Meeting
  • Notes synced to Hub → Summaries → My Notes within seconds of meeting end
  • Full-text search across all past notes from all meetings
  • Filter by: tag, date range, meeting title, person mentioned
  • Export: markdown copy, push to Docs, email to self, download PDF
  • Notes linked to recording: click timestamp → player jumps to that moment
  • Share individual notes or full note set via link (view or edit access)
