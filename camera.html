AI Avatar (Stylized)

A stylized animated character that mirrors your expressions — or presents your slide decks with a typed script in any language. No camera required for Clips.

Stylized Avatar Types
Illustrated
  • Flat 2D illustration style
  • Clean, professional look
  • Best for corporate meetings
  • Customizable skin and hair tone

  • Animated manga-style character
  • More expressive emotions
  • Popular for creative teams

3D Cartoon
  • 3D rendered cartoon character
  • Closest to Realistic avatar feel
  • More CPU-efficient than Realistic
  • Full expression range
Presentation Avatar for Clips
  • Create a Clip without turning on your real camera
  • Upload or paste a script → your avatar reads it aloud with lip sync
  • Select language: avatar speaks in 30+ supported TTS languages
  • Voice cloning option: use a recording of your voice as the TTS voice
  • Pair with a slide deck: avatar narrates each slide automatically
  • Useful for creating training videos, product demos, multilingual content
  • Share the Clip just like any other recording — with analytics
Setting Up Your Stylized Avatar
  1. Go to Settings → Video → AI Avatar → Create Stylized Avatar
  2. Customize appearance — skin tone, hair, outfit, accessories
  3. Preview the avatar with your live camera feed — check expression tracking
  4. Save. Activate from the Video menu in any meeting or Clip recording.
