SMS from Business Number

Send and receive SMS messages from your business phone number — threaded conversations in the FasaFas app, MMS for images, and AI-suggested replies for faster responses.

SMS from Your Business Number
  • All SMS from and to your assigned business number appear in the Phone → Messages tab
  • Threaded view: each contact's conversation is a persistent thread
  • Read status: see when the recipient has read your message (if supported by their carrier)
  • Assign conversations: route an SMS thread to a teammate if they should handle it
  • Templates: save common responses as templates for quick reuse
  • Scheduled SMS: write now, send at the optimal time
  • Business hours SMS auto-reply: 'We're closed — we'll reply during business hours'
MMS (Media Messaging)
  • Send images, PDFs, and short videos (under 3MB) as MMS
  • Received MMS media previewed inline in the conversation thread
  • Forward received media to a channel as a file share
  • Emoji in SMS: sent as Unicode — renders correctly on all modern smartphones
  • Group SMS: send to up to 10 recipients — creates an MMS group thread
  • Note: MMS rates apply and are separate from voice call minutes
AI in SMS
  • AI Receptionist: auto-reply to inbound SMS during off-hours with intelligent responses (see AI Receptionist feature)
  • Suggested replies: AI suggests 3 reply options based on the inbound SMS context
  • Draft assist: type a rough reply → AI improves grammar and tone
  • AI translates inbound SMS: hover → 🌐 → translate to your language
  • AI flags: urgent messages (callback requested, complaint, compliance issue) → highlighted at top of inbox
  • Sentiment analysis: positive / neutral / negative tag on each conversation for support team reporting
