AI Summarizes Unread Threads

Return to a channel after a week away and AI summarizes what you missed in seconds — decisions made, key discussions, and anything requiring your attention — without reading every message.

Unread Thread SummaryOne-ClickClaude PoweredPrivate to YouCatch-Up
How It Works
Summary Quality
Controls
AI Thread Summaries
  1. You open a channel with unread messages
    FasaFas detects you have 50+ unread messages or have been away for 3+ days
  2. An 'AI Catch-Up' banner appears at the top of the channel
    '📊 You have 127 unread messages. Summarize with AI →' — one button
  3. AI generates a private summary in under 10 seconds
    Claude reads all unread messages and produces: decisions made, key discussions, your mentions, anything requiring action
  4. Summary shown only to you in a collapsible card
    Other channel members don't see it. Dismiss when done. No trace.
What the Summary Covers
📋 Summary Sections
Key DecisionsWhat the group agreed on or decided while you were away
Your MentionsAny message where someone @mentioned you — with context
Action ItemsTasks that were assigned or discussed
Active ThreadsOngoing discussions still being replied to
File SharesImportant documents or images shared in the channel
Questions for YouDirect questions asked that haven't been answered
Controls & Settings
  • Trigger manually: hover channel name → 'Summarize unread messages'
  • Or: right-click any thread → 'Summarize thread'
  • Threshold: configure when AI summary is offered (50+ unread, 3+ days, or always)
  • Admin can enable/disable AI summaries per channel or account-wide
  • Opt out: Settings → AI Companion → Thread Summaries → off
  • Available on: AI Companion add-on and above
