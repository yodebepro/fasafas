Smart Chapters & Actions

Claude segments every meeting transcript into named topic chapters and extracts every action item with suggested assignee and due date — ready in under 20 minutes after the meeting ends.

API
Smart Chapter Generation
  1. Full transcript assembled
    Whisper + diarization complete → labeled transcript is the input
  2. Claude performs topic segmentation
    Identifies significant semantic shifts. Minimum chapter: 3 minutes. Typical: 5–10 chapters per hour.
  3. Titles and summaries generated
    2–6 word title per chapter + 1–2 sentence summary. Example: '0:00 Intros · 8:32 Q3 Budget · 24:10 Roadmap · 41:05 Action Items'
  4. Placed on recording timeline
    Clickable markers on the playback bar. Chapter sidebar in the viewer. Deep link to any chapter.
Action Item Extraction
Detection Patterns
  • Commitment: 'I'll…', 'I will send…', 'I'm going to…'
  • Assignment: 'Can you [name]…', '[Name] will handle…', 'Action for [name]:'
  • Deadline: 'by Friday', 'end of day', 'next sprint', 'before the 15th'
  • Decision-action pair: 'We decided [X] — [Name] will lead it'

Action Item Structure
{"task":"Send budget to board","assignee":"Ana Lopez","due":"2026-05-07","source":"by next Wednesday","timestamp":1842,"confidence":"high"}
  • One-click 'Push All to Tasks' sends every item at once
  • Integrates with Asana, Jira, Monday.com, Notion
  • Assignee notified with task + meeting link
