HIPAA BAA

FasaFas offers a HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for healthcare organizations — enabling use of FasaFas for telehealth, patient discussions, and any communication involving Protected Health Information.

HIPAA BAA Details
What's Covered
Technical Safeguards
HIPAA Business Associate Agreement
  • FasaFas is a Business Associate (BA) when you (a Covered Entity or other BA) use FasaFas to create, receive, transmit, or maintain PHI
  • BAA available on: Enterprise plan
  • To request a BAA: contact sales@fasafas.app or your account manager
  • BAA process: legal review → FasaFas standard BAA or your organization's BAA template → signature → activated on your account
  • Subcontractors: FasaFas's BAA with you extends to FasaFas's subprocessors (AWS, Twilio, etc.) — all signed BAAs available on request
What the BAA Covers
Permitted with BAA
  • Telehealth video visits via FasaFas meetings
  • Discussing patient cases in private channels or DMs
  • Sharing PHI-containing files in secure channels
  • AI transcription of telehealth sessions (with HIPAA logging)
  • Recording telehealth sessions with appropriate consent
NOT Covered (Use with Care)
  • AI features that send data to third-party models require additional BAA review
  • Public channels: PHI should never be shared in public channels regardless of BAA
  • Guest/unauthenticated access: attendees without FasaFas accounts may not be covered
Technical Safeguards for HIPAA
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256 for all stored data (recordings, transcripts, files, messages)
  • Encryption in transit: TLS 1.3 for all API traffic, DTLS-SRTP for media
  • Audit logging: comprehensive access logs for all PHI-related actions — available for audit
  • Access controls: role-based access, MFA enforcement, automatic session timeout (configurable: 15–30 min for HIPAA accounts)
  • Breach notification: FasaFas will notify you of any breach within 60 days as required by HIPAA
  • Training: FasaFas staff with PHI access receive annual HIPAA training
