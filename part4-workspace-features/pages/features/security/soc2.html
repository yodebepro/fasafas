SOC 2 Type II Certification
  • SOC 2 Type II: independent third-party audit by a licensed CPA firm (Ernst & Young)
  • Type II vs Type I: Type I is a point-in-time snapshot. Type II covers a 12-month period — proving controls work consistently.
  • Annual: FasaFas undergoes this audit every year — report renewed annually
  • Audit firm: Ernst & Young — one of the Big Four accounting firms
  • Observation period: 12 continuous months ending the prior December
  • Scope: cloud infrastructure, access controls, change management, incident response, availability, and confidentiality

Accessing the SOC 2 Report
  • Available to: Business and Enterprise plan customers on request
  • Request: email security@fasafas.app with your account ID and a brief statement of purpose
  • NDA: FasaFas requires a signed NDA before sharing the report (standard industry practice)
  • Turnaround: NDA and report shared within 2 business days
  • Third-party risk reviews: FasaFas security team available to answer questions or join a vendor assessment call
  • Penetration test summary: executive summary of annual pen test also available on request (full report under NDA)
