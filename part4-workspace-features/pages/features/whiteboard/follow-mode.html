Follow Mode & Presenter Mode

Host enables Follow Mode to lock all participants' viewports to yours — everyone sees exactly what you're showing, as you pan and zoom. Participants can break free anytime if needed.

Follow Mode (Voluntary)
  • Any participant can voluntarily follow any other participant's viewport
  • Click presence avatar → Follow [Name] → your view syncs to theirs
  • Their panning and zooming becomes your panning and zooming
  • A 'Following [Name]' indicator appears at the top of your canvas
  • Exit: click 'Stop Following' in the top bar or press Escape
  • You can still interact with the canvas while following — your cursor moves independently even as your view follows theirs
Presenter Mode (Host-Controlled)
  • Host only: Whiteboard toolbar → Present → Start Presenter Mode
  • All participants' viewports lock to the host's view — even if they click elsewhere on the canvas
  • Participants see a banner: 'Presenter: Ana — [Stop Following]'
  • Participants can click 'Stop Following' to break free if they need to check something
  • Host zooms into a specific area → all participants' viewports zoom identically
  • Host can spotlight their cursor: cursor appears larger and highlighted for all to see
  • End presenter mode: host clicks 'Stop Presenting' → all viewports unlock
Whiteboard + Video Meeting Combined
  • FasaFas meeting → More → Whiteboard → opens in a side panel alongside video
  • Presenter Mode works while in the meeting — you're presenting visually AND your video/audio are live
  • Screen share alternative: instead of sharing your screen, use Whiteboard + Presenter Mode — everyone sees the same whiteboard without screen sharing lag
  • Laser pointer works in both contexts
  • Participants can still take notes or use chat while following the whiteboard presenter
