Smart Connectors (Auto-Route)

Smart connectors that auto-route around obstacles, snap to shape connection points, and update their paths automatically when you move shapes — no manual rerouting ever needed.

Creating Smart Connectors
  1. Hover over a shape
    Blue connection dots appear on the edges
  2. Click and drag from a dot
    A connector line starts from that connection point
  3. Drag to another shape
    Connection snaps to the nearest connection point on the target shape
  4. Release to connect
    Connector is live — it will follow both shapes if either moves
  5. Or: use the Connector tool (press C)
    Click shape A then click shape B — connector created automatically between them
Connector Routing Styles
🔗 Routing Options
Orthogonal (default)90-degree turns only — standard flowchart style. Auto-routes around shapes.
CurvedSmooth bezier curves — organic look for concept maps and mind maps.
ElbowL-shaped connector — one bend point, manually draggable.
StraightDirect line between two points — no routing around obstacles.
DynamicAuto-switches between orthogonal and curved based on canvas density.
Labels & Arrowheads
  • Add label: double-click any connector → type text → label appears centered on the connector
  • Label moves with the connector — stays centered when shapes move
  • Arrowhead options: none, open arrow, filled arrow, diamond, circle, square
  • Bidirectional: arrowheads on both ends — for two-way relationships
  • Line style: solid, dashed, dotted — matches your diagram's convention
  • Color: connector color and label color set independently
