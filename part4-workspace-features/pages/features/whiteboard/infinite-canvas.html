Infinite Canvas (Pan & Zoom)

An infinite, zoomable canvas that scales from sticky-note brainstorms to multi-team architecture diagrams — pan with your mouse or trackpad, zoom from 10% to 400%, and navigate with a minimap.

Performance
Infinite Canvas
  • The canvas extends infinitely in all directions — no page size or boundary
  • Objects created anywhere on the canvas — drag to position
  • Selection: click an object, or drag a selection box around multiple objects
  • Move: drag selected objects to any position
  • Resize: drag corner or edge handles — hold Shift to constrain aspect ratio
  • Copy/paste: Ctrl+C / Ctrl+V — pastes near the original position
  • Undo/Redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y — full history throughout the session
Navigation Controls
🧭 Navigation Methods
PanClick and drag on empty canvas area · or two-finger drag on trackpad · or hold Space + drag
ZoomMouse scroll wheel · trackpad pinch · or use the zoom control (bottom right)
Zoom to fitPress Cmd+Shift+H (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+H (Win) — fits all objects in view
Zoom to selectionSelect objects → Cmd+Shift+F — zoom to show only selected objects
MinimapBottom-right corner shows full canvas overview — click to navigate · drag the viewport rectangle
Go to originPress Cmd+0 (Mac) / Ctrl+0 (Win) — snaps back to the center of the canvas
Performance at Scale
  • Virtual rendering: only objects in the current viewport are rendered — off-screen objects don't impact performance
  • Tested with up to 10,000 sticky notes, shapes, and connectors on a single canvas
  • Object level-of-detail: at low zoom, complex objects simplify to bounding box representations
  • Collaboration scale: up to 50 simultaneous editors with CRDT sync
  • Read-only viewers: unlimited — no performance impact for viewers-only
  • Auto-archiving: canvases inactive for 90 days moved to cold storage (still accessible, slower to load)
